About Qili International Trading

Shenzhen Qili Trading Firm is a B2B toy export company based in Shenzhen, Guangdong Province, China. We specialize in sourcing and exporting high-quality toys from reliable Chinese manufacturers to international wholesalers and importers across North America, Europe, Australia, and other global markets.

Our product catalog includes over 560 items spanning 14 categories: building blocks and construction sets, action figures, dolls and plush toys, electronic and interactive toys, educational toys, outdoor and sports toys, arts and crafts, puzzles and board games, vehicles, and more. We work closely with manufacturing partners to ensure products meet international quality and safety standards including EN71, ASTM F963, and CPSIA.

Our Services

Product Sourcing

We source from trusted factories across China, handling supplier evaluation, price negotiation, and quality assurance for every product in our catalog.

OEM & Customization

We offer flexible OEM solutions including custom packaging, branding, logo printing, and product modifications to meet your market requirements.

Export & Logistics

Full export service from factory to destination — including quality inspection, container loading, customs clearance, and international shipping (FOB, CIF).

Compliance & Certification

We ensure all products meet target market regulations. We coordinate third-party testing for EN71 (EU), ASTM F963 (US), CPSIA, and other required certifications.

Digital Marketing & Technology

As part of our commitment to reaching international B2B buyers efficiently, Qili uses modern digital marketing tools and advertising technology to promote our products and connect with potential wholesale customers worldwide.

Google Ads for B2B Marketing

We use Google Ads to run targeted Search campaigns aimed at international toy buyers searching for wholesale building block suppliers, toy exporters from China, and bulk toy distributors. Our advertising strategy focuses on high-intent B2B keywords to connect with importers, distributors, and retailers looking for quality toy products at competitive wholesale prices.

Internal Advertising Management Tool

To streamline our advertising operations, we have developed an internal Python-based tool that integrates with the Google Ads API to programmatically manage our advertising campaigns. This tool is used exclusively by our company to manage our own single Google Ads account. Key capabilities include:

  • Campaign Creation: Programmatically create and configure Search campaigns with proper targeting, budgets, and bidding strategies.
  • Campaign Management: Manage ad groups, keywords (broad, phrase, exact match), and responsive search ads through our Python interface.
  • Performance Reporting: Generate daily and weekly performance reports (impressions, clicks, conversions, cost, ROI) to optimize advertising spend.

The tool is built on the Google Ads API Python client library and authenticates via a Google Cloud Service Account. It is an internal-only tool — not distributed or sold to any external parties. Our estimated daily API usage is under 100 operations.

Analytics & Tracking

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to track website traffic, user behavior, and conversion metrics. Combined with our advertising data, this helps us understand which products attract the most buyer interest and optimize our marketing strategy accordingly.
